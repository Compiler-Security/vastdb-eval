## Root Cause

The vulnerability is a Use-After-Free in `bad.c`. The `qualified_name` pointer is allocated by `make_qualified_name()` and stored in `member_filedata->file_name` at line 529 (for thin archives). After processing, `close_file(member_filedata)` is called at line 534, and later `free(qualified_name)` is called at line 587. However, `member_filedata->file_name` still holds the address of the freed `qualified_name` pointer. When subsequent error() calls (such as at lines 373, 492, 501) use `filedata->file_name` as the message argument, they dereference freed memory. The `error()` function at line 121 then calls `vfprintf()` at line 130 with this freed pointer, triggering the CWE-416 Use After Free.

## Root Cause

The vulnerability is in the BWDIF video deinterlacing filter. The `config_props()` function at line 467 only validates the luma dimensions (`link->w` and `link->h`), checking that both are at least 3 pixels to ensure the filter algorithm has sufficient data. However, when processing chroma planes (chrominance channels), the dimensions are downsampled using `AV_CEIL_RSHIFT()` at lines 549-550.
